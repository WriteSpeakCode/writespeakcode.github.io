Meetup Format

Write/Speak/Code meetups will rotate focusing on writing, speaking or coding.

General Format

  • Intros — practice your pitch/bio!
  • Lightning Talks
  • Focus Exercises
  • Closing — stay accountable by committing to actions for next month

Write Exercises

  • Topic generation exercises
  • Writing & peer feedback

Speak Exercises

  • Topic generation exercies
  • What's a talk proposal?
  • Research speaking opportunities
  • Write a talk proposal & peer feedback
  • Lightning talks

Code Exercises

  • Share knowledge & find helpers
  • Explore projects
  • Find issues
  • Set up
  • Code!
