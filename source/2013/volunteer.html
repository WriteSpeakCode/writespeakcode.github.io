Want to help out Write/Speak/Code? Here's what we need:
  • Share your talk proposal!
    We are looking for women (and men!) to share real, live talk proposals. The proposals will be reviewed by attendees and conference organizers. Submit your proposal
  • Are you a conference organizer?
    We are looking for conference organizers with experience evaluating talk proposals to:
    • Be on a panel and give feedback on talk proposals on the afternoon of June 21st
    • Give written feedback on talk proposals prior to the conference
    Email us if you're interested.
  • Know a company that wants to empower women developers?
    Share our Sponsor page or email Chrys Wu at sponsor@writespeakcode.com.
  • Help us get free food
    If you know a restaurant, coffee shop, bakery, or other magical place that creates sustenance that might be willing to donate food for lunch or snacks, have them email us.
    Note: due to our venue, we can only accept food donations and not discounts.
