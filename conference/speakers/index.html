    Tracey Welson-Rossman
    • Conference Organizer Panel
    • Talk Proposal Mentoring
    • Speaker Mentoring

    Tracey Welson-Rossman is a Founding Member and Chief Marketing Officer at Chariot Solutions, a leading software and mobile development consulting firm, and the Founder of TechGirlz.org, a non-profit organization dedicated to inspiring teenage girls to consider careers in technology.

    Twice recognized as a top place to work in Philadelphia, Chariot Solutions has grown from its initial staff of 10 to over 50 full time employees, over 90% of whom are technologists. The employees also speak at conferences, write technical articles and contribute to open source projects.

    Tracey's work in the IT world led her to address the dearth of girls believing that technology is a promising career for them. She founded TechGirlz in 2009 to help to address this and is proud to report the organization has worked with over 1700 girls to date.

    Tracey is the founder and co-chair of the annual Emerging Technologies for the Enterprise Conference. ETE attracts world-renowned thought leaders in cutting-edge technologies and serves as a community forum for regional technology companies. As the founder and chair of the Women in Tech Summit, currently in its 4th year. She is also a Founding Board Member of Philadelphia Startup Leaders, dedicated to growing technology startup businesses and the largest and most active community of startup entrepreneurs in the Philadelphia. She was honored as one of 40 Women to Watch over 40 worldwide. She is an advisor to two Philadelphia area startups - Haydle and Roar.

    Mary Scotton
